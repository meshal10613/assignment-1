Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating and implementing new ideas, solutions, methods, or products that add value or improve existing practices. It is a cornerstone of progress in various fields, including technology, business, healthcare, education, and society as a whole. Platforms like Amazon reshaping industries.

Sustainability

Sustainability refers to meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It emphasizes maintaining a balance between environmental, social, and economic factors to ensure long-term health and well-being for people and the planet.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the foundational physical and organizational structures and facilities necessary for the functioning of a economy. It includes buildings, roads, bridges, water supply, energy systems, communication networks, and more. Infrastructure is essential for providing services, and enhancing the quality of life.

Empowerment

Empowerment refers to the process of enabling individuals or groups to take control of their own lives, make informed decisions, and achieve their full potential. It often involves providing resources, education, opportunities, and support to overcome barriers and create a sense of self-determination.
